Pork Chops

Ingredients

  • 4 (5 ounce) boneless, center-cut pork chops, 1-inch thick
  • 1 teaspoon Cajun seasoning
  • Â½ cups cheese and garlic croutons

Directions

  1. Step 1

    Preheat an air fryer to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).

  2. Step 2

    Place pork chops on a plate and season both sides with Cajun seasoning.

  3. Step 3

    Pulse croutons in a small food processor until fine; transfer to a shallow dish.

    Lightly beat eggs in a separate shallow dish. Working one at a time, dip pork chops

    into beaten egg, letting excess drip off; press into crouton breading to coat both sides

    and place breaded chop, unstacked, onto a plate. Repeat with remaining chops. Mist chops with cooking spray.
